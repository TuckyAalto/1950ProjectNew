  • Work with a template based CMS

    Create an account with either Wordpress or Tumblr. Choose a pre built theme, and make at least two alterations to it (color, font,etc). Add a minimum of three posts. Include the use of text, hyperlinks and images. The content of one of the three posts must include a list of what parts of the theme you changed. The content of the remaining posts may be anything you like. Use a latin generator to create generic content if you wish (http://www.lipsum.com/). Provide a link on your homepage to your Tumblr or Wordpress blog page.
